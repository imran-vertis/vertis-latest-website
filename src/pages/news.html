Vertis Team Member Wins Sitecore Most Valuable Professional Award

January 15, 2024

SINGAPORE - Singapore-headquartered digital agency Vertis today announced that Thom Puiman, Vertis Solution Architect, has been named a Most Valuable Professional (MVP) in the Technology sector by Sitecore®, a global leader in end-to-end digital experience management software. Thom was one of only 147 worldwide to be named a Sitecore Technology MVP this year.
